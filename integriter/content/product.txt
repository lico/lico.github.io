			<h3>What does Integriter enable you to do? </h3>
			<p>
				Integriter simplifies the management of your timestamps for long-term validity, 
				allowing you to focus on your work without the need to worry about technical aspects or time constraints
			</p>
			<ul>
				<li>Ensure the integrity of your files by generating trusted timestamps from our reputable Timestamp Service Authorities (TSA)</li>
				<li>Maintain the integrity of your timestamps by easily verifying their validity at any time, even after their initial generation</li>
				<li>Securely store your timestamp data for the long-term by utilizing Integriter.com's reliable and secure storage solution</li>
				<li>Efficiently manage your timestamps by defining retention policies that align with your specific needs and requirements</li>
				<li>Ensure the ongoing validity of your timestamps over time by re-stamping them automatically</li>
			</ul>
			<p>&nbsp;</p>
			Some features are free and can be used without creating a user account:
			<ul class="bltlst">
				<li ><a href="/tools/timestampAttestation.html">Obtain free timestamp attestations</a> from a Timestamp Service Authority (TSA) for your files,</li>          
				<li><a href="/tools/timestampChecker.html">Verify the authenticity of your timestamps</a> using the request and response files provided.</li>
			</ul>
